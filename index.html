Research Questionnaire

This study explores the awareness and perceptions of hospitality students regarding AI-driven mood detection and neurogastroenterological food recommendation in hotel dining. The questionnaire takes approximately 10 to 15 minutes to complete.

Before you begin, please note:
  • Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any point without consequence.
  • Your responses are completely anonymous. No names or identifying information will be collected.
  • All data will be used exclusively for academic research purposes and stored securely.
  • This study has been authorised by the International Hotel and Tourism Institute (IHTI), Nairobi.
  • If you have any questions, contact the researcher: Brandis Kool, IHTI Nairobi.

Section C

Awareness of AI-Driven Mood Detection Technologies

Computer vision is AI technology that can analyse images and video to identify patterns including human facial expressions and emotions. Affective computing is a branch of AI that enables machines to detect and respond to human emotional states.
